# 1.Introduction: Artificial Intelligence Creative Competition at UESTC

## 1.Competition Objectives

1. Promote deep integration between courses and academic competitions, transforming course evaluation methods.
2. Inspire innovation, encouraging participants to "dare to think, dare to create, and dare to win."
3. Utilize AI knowledge to explore challenging problems in human society and design corresponding solutions.
4. Develop skills starting from information retrieval, including analysis, design, expression, and critical thinking.
5. Serve as the seed for academic competitions, the blossom of scientific research, and the fruit of commercial products.

## 2.Registration Requirements

- There are no restrictions on participants' majors.
- The competition is an individual contest.
- Each submitted work must have a designated advisor.

## 3.Project Requirements

- The submitted project must focus on artificial intelligence technology and explore creative application solutions with real-world implementation potential.
- The competition adopts an open-ended topic approach, and implementation of the project is not required during the competition phase.
- Participants are encouraged to fully exercise their creativity, freely explore application scenarios, and gather relevant materials.
- The final submission should be an original work that provides a detailed introduction to the creative proposal.

## 4.Competition Format

### 4.1 Preliminary Round

- Participants must submit a project proposal, including a project overview, background analysis, application scenarios, problem-solving approach, working principles, and key innovations.

### 4.2 Final Round

- Participants will present their projects through a pitch presentation using videos or PPT slides.
- The presentation and Q&A session will last 7 minutes.

### 4.3 Evaluation Criteria

- The evaluation will be based on three aspects: topic selection, solution, and presentation.
- Detailed evaluation criteria and scoring breakdown are provided in the final section.

## 5.Advancement Rules

### 5.1 Judges

- The judging panel will primarily consist of AI experts, ensuring a fair and impartial evaluation process.
- The competition organizing committee will oversee the procedures and ensure compliance.
- The preliminary round will be conducted online, while the final round will involve an in-person presentation.

### 5.2 Preliminary Round

- The advancement ratio will be determined based on the number of registered participants.
- Experts will review submissions online and select finalists.
- Each project will be scored by three experts, and the final score will be the average of these evaluations.
- Projects will be ranked based on total scores, with detailed scoring criteria provided in the appendix.

### 5.3 Final Round

- The final round will involve live presentations and Q&A sessions.
- A panel of 3-5 professional judges will score the projects to determine the final winners.
- Each project will be scored by five experts, and the final score will be the average of these evaluations.
- Projects will be ranked based on total scores, with detailed scoring criteria provided in the appendix.

## 6.Registration and Timeline

**Submission Requirements:**

- Participants must submit registration materials and competition entries as per the guidelines.

**Schedule:**

| Week       | Event Schedule                                         |
| ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Week 12-13 | Registration and preliminary round submission deadline |
| Week 14-16 | Final round presentations                              |
| Week 17    | Announcement of winners                                |





# 2.介绍：2020电子科技大学人工智能创意赛

## 1.竞赛目的
1)推动课程与学科竞赛深度融合，课程评价方式转变等
2)激发创新意识，做到“敢想、敢创、敢赢
3)运用AI知识挖掘人类社会中的挑战性问题并能设计出相应的解决方案
4)从检索信息开始，提升分析、设计能力、表达能力、思辨能力等
5)成为学科竞赛的种子、科学研究的花朵、商业产品的果实

## 2.报名要求
参赛队员不限专业
个人赛
每份参赛作品须有一名指导老师

## 3.作品要求
参赛作品须围绕人工智能技术，探索有具体落地场景的技术应用创意方案。
竟赛采用开放命题，大赛阶段不要求作品实现。参赛者应充分发挥创新能力，自由探索应用场景并获取相关材料，最终提交具有原创性并对创意方案进行详尽介绍的参赛作品。

## 4.赛制说明
### 4.1初赛阶段
参赛者须按要求提交项目创意书，内容应包括参赛作品简介、背景分析、应用场景、解决的痛点问题、工作原理，创新点等
### 4.2决赛阶段
参赛者通过路演汇报的形式结合视频或PPT展示作品，答辩时间为7分钟，接受评委提问。
### 4.3作品评审标准
按照选题，方案及展示三方面进行打分，具体指标及分值见最后一节。

## 5.晋级规则
### 5.1评委
评审以人工智能领域专家为主，秉持公平、公正原则进行评审
竞赛组织委员会负责相关流程的组织和监督。
初赛采取线上评审方式，决赛采取现场答辩的评审方式。
### 5.2初赛
初赛根据报名队伍数量情况确定晋级比例，经过专家在线评审推举决赛晋级队伍。
所有作品由三位专家分别打分，最终得分取平均数，并按总得分从高到低进行排名，评分标准见附录。
### 5.3复赛
决赛按入围队伍现场路演答辩情况,由3-5位专业评委打分评选出最终获奖名单
所有作品由五位专家分别打分，最终得分取平均数，并按总得分从高到低进行排名，标准见附录。

## 6.报名及时间安排

报名材料及参赛作品提交方式：
时间 赛程安排
12周-13周左右 报名及初赛材料提交截止
14-16周 决赛现场答辩
17周左右 公布获奖名单
